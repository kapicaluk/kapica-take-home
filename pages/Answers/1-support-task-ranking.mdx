## Support Task Ranking

#### 5 most favorite support tasks 

1. Dig through logs to troubleshoot a customer's broken project 

2. Identify, file (and, where possible, resolve) bugs in private and public Vercel/Next.js repos on GitHub

> I thrive at problem-solving, enjoying the challenge of analyzing symptoms, pinpointing issues, and developing solutions  


3. Work with the product team to develop a new feature based on feedback from customers

4. Work with engineering teams during incidents and provide updates to internal and external stakeholders

> Finding product inefficiencies using data and improving product reliability by learning from incidents has been a significant part of my time at Netflix, and I have had success in this field. 

5. Act as a dedicated CSE for a handful of key customers to ensure their success using Vercel

> I enjoy establishing strong relationships with customers and cross-functional partners. I have excelled in this area and consider it a critical component of any product development process.

#### 5 least favourite support tasks

1. Analyze hundreds of support tickets to spot trends the product team can use

> While this aligns well with point #3, I sense there's an opportunity for process improvement. I strive to reduce manual tasks, and in this case, I aim to set up dashboards that could deliver the desired context without the need for manual ticket grooming.

2. Manage a support team

3. Run ticket review sessions to make sure tone is consistent

4. Scheduling time-off coverage and collaborating as part of a growing cohesive support team

> I can see myself finding ways to increase team productivity. However, I thrive best in environments that adopt a collaborative leadership style, where decisions are made through teamwork and consensus-building, and where teammates are trusted and granted a certain level of autonomy

5. Respond to 25+ support requests via email every day

> While I appreciate the importance of handling high volumes efficiently, I thrive on variety and intellectual challenges that go beyond repetitive tasks
